Real Madrid Club de Fútbol,simply Real Madrid,byname Los Blancos or in Spanish “the White” is a biggest Spanish professional football club based in Madrid,Spain. Our identity with our color, Playing in all-white uniforms, which led to its nickname “Los Blancos,” Real Madrid is one of the world's best-known teams, with fans worldwide.

MARDID

Record breaking times,Real Madrid has won more Spanish top-division (La Liga) championships (33) than any other Spanish side. The club has also won the Copa del Rey, the main Spanish cup competition, 19 times, as well as nine Spanish Super Cups and two Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) Cups (1985 and 1986).

achievements

From the late 1990s Real Madrid spent enormous sums on luring some of the world's most famous foreign players to the club, where they are known as galácticos (“superstars”). Those players were often the most expensive (by transfer fee) footballers in the world and included such stars as David Beckham, Luis Figo, Ronaldo, Zinedine Zidane, Kaká, and Cristiano Ronaldo.
